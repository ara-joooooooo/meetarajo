Hi, I'm Ara

Hi, I'm Ara! I am a passionate storyteller with a focus on journalism. I am currently studying at Newhouse, where I am honing my skills in digital media, writing, and multimedia production. My goal is to become a content creator who tells compelling stories that resonate with audiences. I am also interested in exploring data journalism and the role of social media in modern storytelling. In the future, I hope to work for a major media organization or start my own independent platform to share stories that matter.

I’m especially interested in stories that sit at the intersection of culture, identity, and everyday life. I enjoy digging deeper than the surface, building trust with the people I cover, and creating work that reflects their voices with honesty and care. For me, journalism isn’t just about reporting facts — it’s about context, connection, and understanding.

Outside of my academic work, I’m constantly experimenting with new tools and storytelling techniques, from short-form video to interactive media. I’m always looking for opportunities to collaborate, learn, and push creative boundaries while staying grounded in strong journalistic values.
